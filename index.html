üö® Disaster Risk Reduction Management Guide

Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill (NSED)

Follow instructions from DRRMHP.

In Case of Fire: RACE

R - Rescue: Rescue persons in immediate danger.

A - Alarm: Activate the nearest fire alarm and call emergency hotline.

C - Confine: Close doors and windows.

E - Evacuate/Extinguish: Attempt to extinguish only if safe. Otherwise evacuate.

Using a Fire Extinguisher: PASS

P - Pull the pin. A - Aim at base. S - Squeeze the handle. S - Sweep side to side.
