üö® Disaster Risk Reduction Management Guide

Essential safety procedures for our building. Please be guided, especially during drills and emergencies.

Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill (NSED) Procedure

Please follow the instructions from the Disaster Risk Reduction Management in Health Program (DRRMHP).

In Case of Fire: **RACE** Protocol

R - Rescue: Rescue persons in immediate danger. Do not put yourself at risk.

A - Alarm: Activate the nearest fire alarm and call the Emergency Hotline (e.g., UP Manila: 141).

C - Confine: Close doors and windows to contain the fire/smoke.

E - Evacuate/Extinguish: Attempt to extinguish the fire only if it is small and safe to do so. Otherwise, proceed immediately to the designated muster area.

Using a Fire Extinguisher: **PASS**

P - Pull: Pull the pin.

A - Aim: Aim the nozzle at the base of the fire.

S - Squeeze: Squeeze the handle to release the agent.

S - Sweep: Sweep from side to side at the base of the fire.
